## About WCCOMPS

The Western Coalition of Competitions (WCCOMPS) is a premier organization dedicated to developing the next generation of cybersecurity professionals through world-class competitions, comprehensive training, and community support.

### Our Mission

WCCOMPS exists to provide students with hands-on, real-world cybersecurity experiences that prepare them for successful careers in the field. We believe that practical competition experience, combined with mentorship and education, creates exceptional security professionals who can defend organizations against evolving threats.

## What We Do

### World-Class Competitions

We organize and run several premier cybersecurity competitions across the Western United States and Pacific Rim:

**WRCCDC** - The Western Regional Collegiate Cyber Defense Competition challenges students to defend corporate networks against professional red teams

**PRCCDC** - Serving 

**CIRCUS** - Digital forensics and incident response competition focusing on investigation skills

**HackerCamp** - Intensive summer training program providing comprehensive security education

### Student Support & Education

Beyond competitions, WCCOMPS provides extensive support to students:

**Free workshops and training sessions** covering fundamental and advanced security topics

**Mentorship programs** connecting students with industry professionals

**Competition preparation resources** including practice scenarios and tool training

**Career guidance and networking** opportunities with potential employers

**Scholarship and financial support** to reduce barriers to participation

### Community Building

We work to strengthen the cybersecurity education community:

**Assistance to other competition organizers** sharing our experience and resources

**Collaboration with academic institutions** to improve cybersecurity curricula

**Industry partnerships** connecting education with real-world needs

**Open source contributions** making our tools and materials available to all

## Why WCCOMPS Matters

### Bridging the Skills Gap

The cybersecurity industry faces a significant talent shortage. WCCOMPS helps address this by:

- Providing practical experience that goes beyond classroom theory
- Identifying and developing talent early in students' academic careers
- Connecting qualified students with employers seeking skilled professionals

### Elevating Standards

Our competitions set the bar for quality cybersecurity education:

- Realistic scenarios based on actual security incidents and threats
- Professional-grade infrastructure mirroring enterprise environments
- Expert red teams providing genuine adversary simulation
- Comprehensive feedback helping students learn and improve

### Creating Opportunities

We remove barriers and create pathways to success:

- Free or low-cost participation to ensure accessibility
- Travel assistance for teams that need financial support
- Exposure to diverse career paths in cybersecurity
- Recognition that helps students stand out to employers

## Our Impact

Since our founding, WCCOMPS has:

- Trained **thousands of students** in practical cybersecurity skills
- Helped **hundreds of students** launch successful security careers
- Supported **dozens of schools** in building competitive security programs
- Contributed to **numerous open-source projects** used by the community
- Assisted **other regional competitions** in improving their events

- Volunteer recruitment and training
- Rule development and enforcement

- White team operations

**Resources & Documentation**
- Competition templates and checklists
