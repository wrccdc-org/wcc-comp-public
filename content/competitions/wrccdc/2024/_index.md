## WRCCDC 2024

### Competition Theme
**Financial Services Security**

The 2024 WRCCDC challenged teams to secure and operate a regional bank's IT infrastructure while under attack from a professional red team and handling critical business operations.

### Competition Details

**Qualifier**
- Date: February 10, 2024
- Format: Online
- Participants: 32 teams
- Top 8 advanced to regional

**Regional Competition**
- Dates: March 22-24, 2024
- Location: Coastline Community College, Garden Grove, CA
- Participants: 8 teams
- Format: In-person, 3-day competition

### Scenario Details

Teams were tasked with operating "Pacific Regional Bank," including:

**Critical Services:**
- Online banking web application
- Core banking database systems
- ATM network management
- Email and internal communication
- DNS and network infrastructure
- Customer service phone system

**Business Injects:**
- Regulatory compliance audits
- Emergency customer account freezes
- New service deployments
- Executive presentations
- Vendor coordination
- Crisis communications

**Red Team Activities:**
- Initial access through phishing simulation
- Privilege escalation attempts
- Data exfiltration scenarios
- Ransomware deployment
- Supply chain compromise simulation
- Insider threat scenarios

### Competition Statistics

- **Total Attack Attempts:** 4,247
- **Successful Defenses:** 3,891 (91.6%)
- **Average Service Uptime:** 94.3%
- **Business Injects Completed:** 156 of 168 (92.9%)
- **Total Competition Hours:** 24 hours active competition
- **Red Team Members:** 18 professional penetration testers
