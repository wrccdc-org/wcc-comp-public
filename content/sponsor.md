## Why Sponsor WCCOMPS?

### Access to Elite Talent

Our competitions attract the best and brightest cybersecurity students:

- **Direct recruitment opportunities** through sponsor booths and networking events
- **Resume access** to competitors (with their permission)
- **Internship and job posting** promotion to our community
- **Brand visibility** among future security professionals

### Meaningful Brand Exposure

Reach the cybersecurity community through multiple channels:

- **Competition branding** including banners, shirts, and promotional materials
- **Website recognition** with logo placement and sponsor profiles
- **Social media mentions** across our platforms
- **Press releases** highlighting your support
- **Speaking opportunities** at competitions and events

### Industry Leadership

Demonstrate your commitment to the cybersecurity community:

- **Thought leadership** by supporting education and workforce development
- **Corporate social responsibility** through meaningful community investment
- **Industry relationships** with academic institutions and other sponsors
- **Innovation partnerships** through collaboration with students and faculty

### Tax Benefits

WCCOMPS is a recognized educational organization:

- **Tax-deductible donations** (consult your tax advisor)
- **Receipt and documentation** for your contribution
- **Non-profit transparency** with clear reporting on fund usage

## FAQ

**Q: Can we sponsor a specific competition?**  
A: Yes! We offer competition-specific sponsorship options. Contact us to discuss.

**Q: How do we access competitor resumes?**  
A: Competitors opt-in to share their resume with sponsors. We provide a resume book after each major competition.

**Q: Can we send volunteers to help with red team activities?**  
A: Absolutely! This is a popular benefit. Your volunteers will need to complete our training program.

**Q: Is our sponsorship tax-deductible?**  
A: WCCOMPS operates as an educational organization. Consult your tax advisor about deductibility for your specific situation.

**Q: When should we commit to sponsorship?**  
A: We accept sponsors year-round, but committing early in the season (by November) ensures maximum visibility and benefits.
