Who We Are

The Western Coalition of Competitions (WCCOMPS) is the premier organization dedicated to developing the next generation of cybersecurity professionals through hands-on competitions, comprehensive training, and community support.

Premier Competitions

We organize WRCCDC, PRCCDC, CIRCUS, and HackerCamp - competitions that set the standard for cybersecurity education.

Student Success

Thousands of students trained, hundreds launched into successful security careers, with hands-on experience that employers value.

Regional Impact

Serving the Western U.S. and Pacific Rim, partnering with universities and industry to strengthen cybersecurity education.
